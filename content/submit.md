# Submit an Article

We're always looking for original student perspectives. Here's how to contribute:

## Submission Guidelines

### Content Requirements
- **Length:** 500-1,500 words
- **Sources:** Minimum 2 credible sources (peer-reviewed journals, academic books, reputable publications)
- **Originality:** Must be your own work, not previously published by someone else
- **Topic:** Must fit Biology, Philosophy, Public Speaking themes or other clubs you want to suggest

### Format
- Use our [article template](article-template.md)
- Include citations in APA format
- Add 2-5 relevant tags
- Choose appropriate category and club

for a detialed guide on how to write an article, see our [Quick Reference Guide](#). *insert google doc link or make it a page on the website* 

### Content Policy
- **Allowed:** Global politics, theoretical discussions, evidence-based arguments
- **Not Allowed:** Local Lebanese politics, plagiarism, unsupported claims, personal attacks

### Review Process
1. **Submit** via form below
2. **Review** by editorial team (3-5 days)
3. **Feedback** sent via email and/or whatsapp
4. **Revision** if needed (2-3 days)
5. **Publication** on website

Average time from submission to publication: **7-10 days**
